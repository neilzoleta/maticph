Who We Are

MATIC is a Filipino-led business process automation studio focused on building streamlined digital solutions for local teams and global players alike.

Soft-launched in 2025, we’re a young team of engineers, analysts, and designers that believes in the power of practical, rapid automation. While we're new to the scene, our team is not new to solving problems. We’ve created internal tools, led process transformation efforts, and have been on the frontlines of operations—so we know what friction looks like.

What We Do

We specialize in automation, but our value goes beyond writing scripts or flows. We work closely with teams to understand what slows them down and design elegant, efficient ways to improve their work experience—from click-saving UI helpers to full-blown reporting pipelines.

Why Choose MATIC?

  • Simple and fast: We design with clarity and function in mind. No bloated dashboards. No extra clicks.
  • Made for people: Our tools are built with your team’s habits in mind—not just technical specs.
  • Culture-driven: As a local startup, we take pride in aligning automation with Filipino workplace culture and ways of working.

Our Vision

To make automation a normal part of how Filipinos work—starting with the simplest things that make the biggest difference.
