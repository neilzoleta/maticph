MATIC Studio Soft Launch

Date: July 5, 2025 (Saturday, Manila)

MATIC Studio officially enters the Philippine tech landscape through its soft launch on July 5, 2025. Proudly Filipino-led, the company is committed to reshaping how businesses operate by delivering meaningful, customized business process automation solutions.

Founded by Neil Zoleta, with strong partnerships alongside Jane Toriaga and Shiela Canent, MATIC Studio brings together a team of visionary professionals who understand the real-world challenges faced by Filipino enterprises.

Purpose-Driven Innovation

At MATIC Studio, we automate what matters. Our mission is to help businesses—big or small—reclaim time, reduce inefficiencies, and enable their teams to focus on what truly matters. By leveraging emerging technology, we design tools that solve real problems with measurable results.

Made by Filipinos, for the WORLD

This is more than just a launch—it's a movement. As a proudly local team, we believe in the power of Filipino talent to lead innovation. MATIC Studio aims to set a standard in accessible, scalable, and impactful automation for Philippine industries and beyond.

What’s Next?

We’re ready to make waves—now it’s all about building momentum and reaching the right partners who share our vision.

Next up: partnering with clients, deploying our automation suite, and delivering solutions powered by skilled Filipino talent—fast, reliable, and built to solve what matters.
