Operational Roadblocks for SMEs

As businesses grow, many processes outpace the capabilities of spreadsheets. What starts as a quick fix often turns into a fragile system—difficult to scale, vulnerable to errors, and risky for critical operations. Spreadsheets can’t ensure data integrity or real-time collaboration at scale, yet many SMEs hesitate to invest in purpose-built solutions due to cost concerns or a lack of technical resources. This gap holds back efficiency, visibility, and growth.
